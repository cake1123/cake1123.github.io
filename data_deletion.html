Requesting Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data from pk, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to info@pressklub.az with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. In the email body, please include:
    • Your full name
    • The email address associated with your account
    • A clear statement requesting the deletion of your data
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once completed

Data Retention

After processing your deletion request, we will remove all personal data associated with your account. Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Contact

For any questions regarding data deletion, please contact info@pressklub.az.
